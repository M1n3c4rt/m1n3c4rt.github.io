Hello! Welcome to the UT/DR Motifs site!
This site showcases all the leitmotif connections between UNDERTALE and DELTARUNE tracks.

This site is best enjoyed with an adblocker and with Javascript JIT enabled on mobile.

showing appropriate credits and attribution.
